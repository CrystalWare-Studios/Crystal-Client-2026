  1. Don't have a Last.fm account? Create a free one at last.fm/join.
    2. 
                            
  2. Connect your music player to Last.fm so it reports what you're playing (called "scrobbling"). If you use Spotify, open the Spotify app on any device, go to Settings → Social → Last.fm, and connect your account. Other players have similar options, or use one of Last.fm's official scrobbler apps.
    3. 
                            
  3. Enter your Last.fm username below and save.
    4. 
                        

                        
                        

                            
                        

                        
This panel updates automatically once something you're scrobbling starts playing.

This only works while you're in the CrystalWare Discord server with your status visible (not set to Invisible).

Spotify offline: Check internet/DNS and reconnect Spotify when online.

Crystal Chatbox setup

            
        

        

            
Welcome to Crystal Chatbox

            
Crystal shows a live message in your VRChat chatbox — your own text, plus music, time, weather, world info, and more if you want it.

            
This takes about a minute. You can change anything later.

        

        

            
Step 1 — Turn on OSC in VRChat

            
In VRChat, open Action Menu → Options → OSC → Enable. If you just turned it on, restart VRChat once.

            
{% if is_android %}Leave the address and port below as-is for on-headset use.{% else %}Leave these as-is unless you're sending to a Quest on your network.{% endif %}

            

                
                
            

        

        

            
Step 2 — Your first message

            
Type the first line you want in your chatbox. Crystal will rotate through your messages automatically — you can add more later.

            
        

        

            
Two ways to send a message

            
    
                
  • Custom Messages — your everyday rotating lines. This runs automatically.
    • 
                
  • Send a Message — sends one message right now, then goes back to your rotation.
    • 
            

        

        

            
Step 3 — Add-ons (optional)

            
The Integrations tab has extras you can turn on any time:

            
    
                
  • Music — shows your current song.
    • 
                
  • Weather and Time — local weather and the clock.
    • 
                
  • Heart Rate, VRChat Instance, VR Battery, and more.
    • 
            

        

        

            
Step 4 — Now Playing (optional)

            {% if spotify_source == "windows_media" %}
            
Nothing to set up — Crystal reads whatever's playing straight from Windows.

            {% else %}
            
Open Integrations → Music to connect Last.fm — takes about a minute.

            {% endif %}
            
You can skip this and do it later.

        

        

            
Step 5 — Make it yours

            
Under Appearance you can add borders, text effects, and a few display modes. That's it for now — the rest you can explore anytime.

        

        

            
All set

            
Crystal will save this and test the connection. If the test fails, make sure OSC is enabled in VRChat and try Test OSC at the top.
